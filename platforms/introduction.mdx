## Sellix for Platforms

Platforms and marketplaces can issue non-custodial and custodial wallets to their merchants, unlocking then all the features of Sellix for their own solutions.

<Info>
  You can see Sellix for Platforms as Stripe Connect but for cryptocurrencies.
  If you'd like to have more information with a 1-on-1 chat, you can [contact our team](mailto:sales@sellix.io).
</Info>

### Custodial or Non-Custodial

Businesses have the option to choose between creating a non-custodial platform or a custodial platform.
They both have their pros and cons and the choice depends on your business needs.

## Get Started

Follow these steps to begin your journey towards offering seamless custodial or non-custodial cryptocurrency solutions to your users.

1. Since access to Sellix for Platforms is currently offered under closed access, the first step is to [get in touch with us](mailto:sales@sellix.io). Our team is ready to discuss your specific needs and how our platform can be tailored to meet them.

2. Whether you're interested in custodial wallets, non-custodial wallets, or a hybrid solution, we'll work closely with you to understand your business model and objectives.

3. We will guide you through the integration process, helping you plan the technical implementation. We'll provide you with the necessary APIs, documentation, and support to make the integration as smooth as possible.

4. Operating within the cryptocurrency space requires adherence to various regulatory standards. We'll assist you in navigating compliance requirements, leveraging our expertise as a licensed and regulated entity.

5. With our support, you'll integrate Sellix's features into your platform. Once implemented, you can begin offering your users advanced cryptocurrency services, complete with the security and efficiency Sellix is known for.

6. After launch, our partnership doesn't end. We offer continuous support to help you optimize the platform, add new features, and scale your operations as your user base grows.
