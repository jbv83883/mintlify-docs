## Cryptocurrencies & Non-Custodial services

Sellix is the leader in the crypto payments & non-custodial services sector.

When you sign up, you are issued a non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet that supports more than 35 unique cryptocurrencies.

The Sellix Wallet is **entirely built in-house**, which enables us to offer a native payment experience across all the [products and services we offer](/introduction#browse-by-product).
